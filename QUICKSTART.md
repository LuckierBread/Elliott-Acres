# 🚀 Quick Start - Elliott Acres on GitHub Pages

Your site is ready to deploy! Follow these simple steps:

## 1. Enable GitHub Pages (30 seconds)

1. Go to your repository: https://github.com/LuckierBread/Elliott-Acres
2. Click **Settings** (top menu)
3. Click **Pages** (left sidebar)
4. Under "Source":
   - Branch: Select `main` (or your current branch)
   - Folder: Select `/ (root)`
5. Click **Save**

**That's it!** Your site will be live in 1-3 minutes at:
### 🌐 https://LuckierBread.github.io/Elliott-Acres/

## 2. Enable Contact Forms (5 minutes)

The contact form needs a free Formspree account:

1. Sign up at https://formspree.io (free)
2. Click "New Form"
3. Name it "Elliott Acres Contact"
4. Copy your form ID (looks like `abc123xyz`)
5. Edit these files in your repository:
   - **contact.html** (line ~33)
   - **blog.html** (line ~35)
6. Replace `your-form-id` with your actual form ID

## 3. Customize Your Site

### Update Contact Information

Search and replace in all HTML files:
- `123 Farm Road, Rural Valley` → Your address
- `(555) 123-4567` → Your phone
- `hello@elliottacres.com` → Your email

### Add Your Photos

Replace images in `static/images/`:
- Keep same filenames, OR
- Update image references in HTML files
