Product Categories

From sweet strawberries to aromatic spices, explore our full range of fresh produce

Strawberries

Sweet, juicy strawberries bursting with flavor. Perfect for eating fresh, making jams, or adding to desserts. Available as fresh berries, frozen, and homemade preserves.

Season: May - July
Varieties: 3+ types available

Asparagus

Tender, crisp asparagus spears harvested at peak freshness. Known for exceptional flavor and texture. Available fresh and pickled with herbs and spices.

Season: April - June
Harvested daily in season

Garlic

Premium hardneck garlic with intense flavor and aroma. Multiple heirloom varieties from mild to bold. Available as bulbs, powder, and seasonal scapes.

Season: July harvest, stored year-round
Multiple heirloom varieties

Seasonal Fruits

A rotating selection of seasonal fruits grown with care. From summer berries to fall apples, tree-ripened for optimal flavor and nutrition.

Season: Various throughout year
Tree-ripened, no artificial ripening

Farm-Fresh Spices

Handcrafted spice blends and dried herbs from our own garden. Each blend is carefully created to enhance your culinary creations with pure, intense flavors of farm-fresh ingredients. No artificial additives, peak potency guaranteed.

Season: Year-round availability
Hand-harvested and dried
Custom blends available
No artificial additives
