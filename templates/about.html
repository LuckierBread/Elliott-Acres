About Elliott Acres

Meet the passionate farmers behind your fresh produce

The Farm

Nestled in the heart of Rural Valley, Elliott Acres spans 25 acres of rich, fertile soil that has been carefully cultivated using sustainable and organic farming practices. Our farm has been in operation since 2018, growing from a simple dream to a thriving agricultural enterprise.

We believe in working with nature, not against it. Our farming methods focus on soil health, biodiversity, and sustainable water management. We use companion planting, natural pest control, and composting to create a balanced ecosystem that produces the healthiest, most flavorful produce possible.

Meet the Farmers

The passionate couple behind Elliott Acres

Serena & Chris Elliott

Serena and Chris Elliott started Elliott Acres with a simple vision: to provide their community with the freshest, most nutritious produce while caring for the land they farm. Both originally from urban backgrounds, they discovered their passion for agriculture during a cross-country trip that introduced them to small-scale farming operations.

Serena brings her background in environmental science to the farm, focusing on sustainable practices and soil health. She manages our composting system and develops our crop rotation plans. Her expertise ensures that our farming methods not only produce great food but also improve the land year after year.

Chris handles the hands-on farming operations, from planting and cultivation to harvest and post-harvest handling. His attention to detail and dedication to quality means every piece of produce that leaves our farm meets the highest standards. He also manages our irrigation systems and equipment maintenance.

Our Values

What drives us every day

Sustainability

We're committed to farming practices that protect and enhance the environment for future generations. Every decision we make considers its long-term impact on the land and ecosystem.

Community

Building strong relationships with our customers and community is at the heart of what we do. We believe in transparency, trust, and supporting local food systems.

Quality

We never compromise on quality. From seed selection to harvest timing, every step is carefully managed to ensure you receive the best possible produce.

Meet Our Farm Family

The animals that make our farm complete

Our Dog

Meet Atlas, the loyal Australian Shepherd of Elliott Acres. With boundless energy and a watchful eye, he herds chickens, greets guests, and guards the land he loves. Every day is an adventure with Atlas by your side.

Cotton and Timber

Our two horses, Cotton and Timber, are the farm's official welcoming committee and weed management team. They help clear overgrown grass areas and their manure makes excellent fertilizer for our crops.

Our Chickens

Our flock of heritage breed chickens provides natural pest control throughout the farm. They help keep insect populations in check while providing rich compost for our soil. Meet Henrietta, Cluck Norris, and the rest of the gang!

Our Commitment to You

When you choose Elliott Acres, you're not just buying produce – you're supporting a vision of sustainable agriculture and local community development.

Pesticide-Free Promise

We never use synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fungicides on any of our crops. Our natural pest management strategies ensure clean, safe produce.

Freshness Guarantee

All of our produce is harvested within 24 hours of pickup or delivery. We believe freshness makes all the difference in taste and nutrition.

Transparency

We welcome visitors to the farm and are always happy to discuss our growing methods. You'll always know exactly how your food was grown.

Want to Visit the Farm?

We love sharing our passion for sustainable farming with others. Contact us to schedule a farm tour, learn about our volunteer opportunities, or just stop by to meet the animals!
