Growing Tips
{% if category == 'Strawberries' %}
  • Store in refrigerator, don't wash until ready to eat
  • Best consumed within 3-5 days of purchase
  • Freeze whole for smoothies
{% elif category == 'Asparagus' %}
  • Store upright in water like flowers
  • Best consumed within 2-3 days
  • Snap ends naturally at breaking point
{% elif category == 'Garlic' %}
  • Store in cool, dry place with good air circulation
  • Don't refrigerate whole bulbs
  • Can last 6-10 months when properly stored
{% elif category == 'Fruits' %}
  • Different fruits have different storage needs
  • Some ripen after picking, others don't
  • We'll provide specific care instructions
{% elif category == 'Spices' %}
  • Store in airtight containers away from light
  • Best used within 1-2 years for optimal flavor
  • Grind whole spices just before use
{% endif %}
